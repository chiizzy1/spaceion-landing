# Design Concept: Spaceion (Series A Redesign)

## The "Neo-Minimalist" Direction

Based on your feedback, we have pivoted to a **Light Mode, Swiss-Style** aesthetic. This design signals "Transparency," "Scale," and "Global Infrastructure."

### 1. The Brand Mark

**Concept:** The "Universal Node". A clean, geometric hexagon representing connected compute.
**Aesthetic:** Bold Black on White. No sci-fi glows. Just structure.

**Key V2/V3 Design Decisions:**

- **Grid:** 12-Column strict alignment.
- **Interaction:** "Floating Code" cards next to the Hero and Features (as seen in the mock).
- **Typography:** Even tighter tracking, more "Poster-like" scaling.

### 4. The "Feature Engine" (The Premium Detail)

**Reference Provided by You:**
We will replace the standard feature list with this exact **Split-Layout Interactive Engine**.

### 5. Why This "Converts"

- **It feels expensive.** White space = Luxury.
- **It feels technical.** Monospace numbers (`01`, `02`) signal "we are engineers."
- **It feels safe.** No weird dark-mode tricks. Just clarity.
